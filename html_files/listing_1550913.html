A private one-bedroom guest unit on the ground level of a three-story townhouse, one block from the 24th Street BART subway station. Located in the heart of the Mission, less than a 10 minute ride from Downtown, Union Square and Embarcadero.

(We live upstairs and have two young kids. Most people say they aren't bothered by the noise that travels downstairs, and we can’t hear you, but if you’re sensitive to noise, this might not be the best fit!)

The space
Looking for a private, spacious and modern place in the heart of the city (and surrounded by great food)—just one block from 24th St BART?

Our modern townhouse is on Valencia and 24th, right in the heart of San Francisco's Mission District, and the ground level is a 1675-square foot guest apartment that's now completely separated from the rest of the house by a stairwell and interior door (not visible in the photos). Stay in our guest unit and you'll enjoy:

Enclosed bedroom
Full bathroom with rainforest shower
Separate entrances
Kitchenette with mini fridge (no stove or oven)
Down pillows and duvet
Queen bed with memory foam mattress (for 2 guests)
Extra deep sofa that doubles as a bed (for 1 extra guest)
TV with Chromecast (no cable!)
